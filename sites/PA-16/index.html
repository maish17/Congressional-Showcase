About Me

AgriTrader started as a question: why do local farmers still rely on phone calls and paper flyers to learn what their crops are worth? That question turned into a full-stack AI application that won the Congressional App Challenge for Pennsylvania's 16th district — and it's the project I'm most proud to have built.

🌾

AgriTrader

AgriTrader is an AI-powered agricultural marketplace and price-forecasting platform built for local farmers. It delivers real-time crop prices, ML-based sell-time recommendations, and a peer-to-peer marketplace — all from a mobile-first app designed to be used in the field, not a boardroom.

The core problem it solves: small-scale farmers often lack access to the same market intelligence that large agricultural operations take for granted. AgriTrader levels that playing field by combining live commodity data, trained machine learning models for corn, soybeans, and wheat, and a UI approachable enough for any farmer — no finance background required.

Predictions come back as confidence-rated buy/hold/sell signals served through a Python/Flask backend, visualized with animated charts on the forecast screen. The ML models are trained on real Pennsylvania agricultural price data.
